Mail Clients

Through the magic of AppleScript, Maildrop works with 3 Major email clients.

Apple Mail


OSX 10.5 & OSX 10.6 : No Known Issues
OSX 10.7 (aka Lion) : Important!:
  • There is a bug in Mail that causes an error when accessing attachments via Applescript, this bug is fixed in the OSX 10.7.2 update, it is highly recommended that you update to 10.7.2 or later.

Outlook 2011


Known issues: Applescript support in Outlook 2011 is currently lacking in a few areas, causing
  • No attachment support (you can't access the actual attachment data via applescript yet)
  • HTML emails may not be extracted correctly, Outlook doesn't provide a plain text version of the email, Maildrop will attempt to extract a plaintext version from the HTML, but this is far from perfect.

Entourage 2008


Known issues: None.
